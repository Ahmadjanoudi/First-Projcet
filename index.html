Fresh Seafood

Fresh Seafood is founded in 1973. It is a fish processing company with a complete range of seafood for everybody: from international wholesale seafood suppliers to a local fish store. Our range of seafood is unique because of it's quality and price. Please take a look at our catalog with: Fresh and Frozen Seafood from the Northsea, Imported fish, Bread-crumbed and deep fried fish products, Vegan Fish products.

About Us

Why Choose Us?

Our company, Sandor Seafood GmbH , was founded in September 2004. We are situated at the heart of the fishing harbor, by the seaside in Bremerhaven. This was the beginning of a big passion that will never end FISH. We started primary with a small assortment of fresh & frozen seafood and developed quickly into a large choice of high-quality seafood-products from all over the world. Daily we supply our customers, trough-out Germany, reliably and flexibly. Even for extraordinary wishes, we would be happy to assist you, providing both support and advice. With our long-term work experience and an excellent suppliers we try to satisfy all requirements as effectively as possible. Our biggest aim: Your contentment. The sustainability of our products is very important to us! We are contributing to the protection of the sea we are MSC-certified.
